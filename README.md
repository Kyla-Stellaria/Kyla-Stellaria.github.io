SB19

-![SB19](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/118173958/202086593-724103b6-86f8-43f4-8eed-feb751351ec8.png)
---
Composed of Pablo (Leader/Main Rapper), Stell (Main Vocalist/Lead Dancer), Josh (Lead Rapper/Sub Vocalist), Ken (Main Dancer/Lead Vocalist) and Justin (Bunso/Sub Vocalist), SB19 is the pioneer of this generation's P-Pop sound that we know and love. Prior to their debut, the boys were trained under the famous Korean Idol system, which focuses on hollistic development.

Given their strong foundation and versatility, the group is always experimenting on different genres to incorporate into their music. This ensures that they always come up with something fresh and exciting to get everyone hyped up and swept off their feet.
